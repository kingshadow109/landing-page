## WearX Differentiation Strategy

### Unique Value Propositions (Draft)

1. **Own Your Data** - Unlike Stitch Fix, users own their wardrobe data
2. **No Subscription** - Pay-per-use or freemium vs monthly fees
3. **Visual-First** - Upload photos vs manual entry like closet apps
4. **AI Outfit Generation** - Not just organization, but recommendations
5. **E-commerce Integration** - Shop from recommendations (future)

### Target Market Gap

**Who WearX Serves Best:**
- Fashion-conscious millennials/Gen Z
- Hate subscription services
- Want to maximize existing wardrobe
- Prefer visual over text-based input
- Price-sensitive but value quality
