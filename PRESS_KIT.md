## Product Information

### WearX App

**Overview:**
The WearX app is a comprehensive health and fitness companion that transforms wearable device data into actionable insights. Available on iOS and Android, the app serves as the central hub for users' health journeys.

**Key Features:**
- **Activity Tracking:** Steps, distance, calories, active minutes
- **Sleep Analysis:** Sleep stages, quality score, trends
- **Heart Rate Monitoring:** 24/7 tracking with resting HR insights
- **Stress Management:** HRV-based stress scoring and breathing exercises
- **Goal Setting:** Personalized targets with progress tracking
- **Community Challenges:** Compete with friends and global leaderboards
- **Premium Insights:** Advanced analytics and personalized coaching (Premium)

**Pricing:**
- Free: Basic tracking and insights
- Premium: $9.99/month or $79.99/year

**Availability:**
- iOS 14+ (App Store)
- Android 8+ (Google Play)

### WearX Devices

**WearX Band:**
- Lightweight fitness tracker
- 14-day battery life
- Water resistant (5ATM)
- Price: $49.99

**WearX Watch:**
- Full-featured smartwatch
- 7-day battery life
- GPS built-in
- Price: $199.99

## Brand Guidelines for Press

### Proper Usage
- Always use "WearX" (capital W, capital X, no space)
- First mention: WearX Technologies Inc. or WearX
- Subsequent mentions: WearX

### Incorrect Usage
- Wear X, Wear-X, wearx, WEARX
- "The WearX" (don't use "the")
