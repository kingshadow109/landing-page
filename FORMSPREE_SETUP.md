# FORMSPREE SETUP GUIDE - Step by Step

## Step 1: Create Formspree Account

1. Go to https://formspree.io/
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"
3. Sign up with:
   - Your email address
   - Or Google account
   - Or GitHub account

## Step 2: Create Your Form

1. After login, click **"+ New Form"**
2. Enter form name: **"WearX Waitlist"**
3. Click **"Create Form"**

## Step 3: Get Your Form Endpoint

After creating, you'll see something like:
```
https://formspree.io/f/xnqkvpzy
```

**Copy this URL** - you'll need it!

## Step 4: Configure Form (Optional but Recommended)

1. Go to **"Settings"** tab
2. Under **"Email Notifications"**:
   - Add your email to receive submissions
3. Under **"Redirect"**:
   - Set success page: `https://symphonious-capybara-0c525d.netlify.app`
4. Click **"Save"**

## Step 5: Test Your Form

1. In Formspree dashboard, click **"Test Form"**
2. Enter a test email
3. Submit
4. Check your email for the submission

## Step 6: Give Me Your Form URL

Once you have your form URL (like `https://formspree.io/f/xnqkvpzy`),
reply with it and I'll update your website!
