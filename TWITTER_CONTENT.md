### Hashtag Rules

**DO:**
- Use 2-3 hashtags max per tweet (Twitter/X best practice)
- Mix branded + community hashtags
- Place at end of tweet or naturally in text
- Test which drive engagement

**DON'T:**
- Hashtag spam (looks desperate)
- Use irrelevant trending hashtags
- Put hashtags before the main message
- Use the same combo every time

## ğŸ’¡ CONTENT TIPS

### Voice & Tone
- **Conversational** - Write like you're texting a friend
- **Confident but humble** - We know our stuff, but we're learning too
- **Visual** - Use emojis to break up text
- **Action-oriented** - Clear CTAs in every post

### Posting Schedule
- **Launch week:** 2-3 posts per day
- **Ongoing:** 1 post per day minimum
- **Best times:** 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-8 PM (test your audience)

### Engagement Tactics
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Ask questions to spark conversation
- Quote tweet with additional insights
- Share user-generated content when available
