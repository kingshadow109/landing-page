# WearX Business Model
## Revenue Streams & Unit Economics

---

## Executive Summary

WearX operates a **freemium subscription model** with multiple revenue streams designed to maximize customer lifetime value while maintaining sustainable unit economics. Our diversified approach reduces dependency on any single revenue source and creates natural upsell pathways.

## üìä Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Channel | CAC | % of Acquisitions |
|---------|-----|-------------------|
| Paid Social (Instagram/TikTok) | $18 | 40% |
| Influencer Marketing | $15 | 25% |
| App Store Optimization | $8 | 15% |
| Referral Program | $6 | 12% |
| Content/SEO | $4 | 8% |
| **Blended CAC** | **$13.50** | 100% |

### Lifetime Value (LTV) Calculation

```
LTV Components:
- Average subscription length: 14 months
- Average monthly subscription: $5.50
- Subscription LTV: $77

- Average affiliate purchases per year: 6
- Average commission per purchase: $12
- Annual affiliate revenue: $72
- 2-year affiliate LTV: $144

- Total LTV: $221

LTV/CAC Ratio: $221 / $13.50 = 16.4:1
```

## üéØ Pricing Strategy

### $4.99 Premium Pricing Rationale

1. **Below Coffee Test:** Less than a daily latte‚Äîeasy mental math
2. **Below Competition:** Stitch Fix starts at $20/styling fee
3. **High Perceived Value:** Unlimited outfits vs. one-time styling
4. **Conversion Optimized:** A/B tested sweet spot for conversion

### Pricing Experiments

| Test | Hypothesis | Expected Impact |
|------|------------|-----------------|
| $3.99 vs $4.99 | Lower price = higher volume | +20% conversion, -15% revenue/user |
| $4.99 vs $6.99 | Premium positioning | -10% conversion, +25% revenue/user |
| Annual discount 20% vs 30% | Optimize for cash flow | Test prepayment vs. retention |

## üîÑ Revenue Model Resilience

### Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|---------------------|
| Subscription fatigue | Flexible pricing, pause options |
| Affiliate commission cuts | Diversify partners, direct brand deals |
| Economic downturn | Emphasize value (save money on bad purchases) |
| Competition | Continuous innovation, data moat |

### Expansion Opportunities

1. **B2B SaaS:** White-label styling for retailers
2. **Enterprise:** Employee styling benefits for corporations
3. **Marketplace:** Direct sales with higher margins
4. **Licensing:** AI technology to fashion brands
